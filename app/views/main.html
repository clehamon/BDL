Qwizard is a fun way to take class quizzes, without the hassle of distributing quizzes on paper. It's the best way to keep students engaged in your lesson, without interrupting your teaching flow.

How it Works:
Teachers can create any kind of quiz in seconds, or choose from an open library of quizzes. The quiz is then shown on a screen or projector. Students connect to your quiz by entering a 4-digit quiz code on their device. Then you perform the quiz at your own pace, and see instant answers from your students. It's a lot of fun and very easy.
